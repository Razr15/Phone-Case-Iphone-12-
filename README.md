# Phone-Case-Iphone-12-
So recently I got a new phone handed down to me (Iphone 12) and wasnt too happy with the case with it so I decided ill just buy a new one but then I thought why buy one when i can just make, so heres a simple iphon 12 case design with a simple diamond shape at the back (You can always change it if you decide you dont like it)
Step by step Instructions -
1 - Make a simple sketch of a phone case with the correct dimensions (change it according to your phone) and extrude it to a width that matches your phone 
2 - Make a sketch inside the cuboid you just made and extrude it inside so there space for the actual phone
3 - Fillet the edges and at the back make a simple sketch where the camera of your phone would be and extrude it
4 - Make a sketch tracing the square you just extruded making sure theres enough space between each line and now extrude it upwards so it sticking out ( fillet the edges)
